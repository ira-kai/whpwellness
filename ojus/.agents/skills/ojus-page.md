# /ojus-page
Writes product and bundle page copy in the established site voice.

Voice rules extracted from existing whpwellness.com posts:
- Vary the opening based on context — sometimes lead with the reader's experience, sometimes with a question, a surprising fact, or the product itself. Match the energy to the topic.
- Validate before explaining
- H2 for major sections, H3 for sub-points
- Bold key terms inline
- Max 3-4 sentences per paragraph
- Inline citations with superscripts, full references at bottom
- Products linked naturally in context, never forced
- Mechanism explained in one sentence per product
- Always end: "best used as part of a comprehensive approach"
- Healthcare provider caveat at close
- 20-25 SEO tags per post

NEVER: amazing, boost, transform, proven, guaranteed, miracle
ALWAYS: supports, may help, evidence suggests, research indicates

All output routes through /ojus-comply before staging.
